Designing Customer Journeys Across Channels

Customers do not experience channels separately, they experience momentum. Demo Dudes maps journeys by intent state and then assigns channel roles: paid ads attract, landing pages clarify, email nurtures, SMS prompts action, and product pages close. Trigger logic prevents message collision and keeps timing coherent. If someone purchases, promotional pressure pauses and onboarding begins. If someone stalls, educational content resumes before sales asks return. This orchestration improves customer experience and boosts conversion because every channel reinforces the same narrative rather than competing for attention.
