The 7-Day Welcome Sequence That Converts

A high-performing welcome journey is less about volume and more about timing clarity. Demo Dudes uses a seven-day sequence where each message has one clear job: orient, educate, personalize, and activate. Day one confirms the subscription and points to a fast-start guide. Day two shares a curated recommendation list. Day three introduces social proof. Day four asks about goals. Day five segments based on behavior. Day six presents a limited offer. Day seven closes with a progress summary and next milestone. The result is better activation and higher second-month retention.
